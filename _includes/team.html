Marc-Andre, bazmarc as he is know in the LEGO community has always had a passion for all thing realted to print, back in 1995 he worked in a sticker factory before he headed out to universtity in Industrial Engineering. Following this, he worked for over 20 years at IBM as a Support Specialist for Power System. In fall of 2018 Marc-Andre chose the entepreneur path. First he became the new owner of Le Flag Shop Montreal (see full story here). Then, at about the same time, he took over Million-Prints.com a small company he used to deal with a lot for all his custom prints on LEGO elements. You see Marc-André is also am AFOL (Adult Fan of LEGO) and the ambassador of the MIDNSTORMS® Community Partners that recently became the ROBOTMAK3RS RLOC (Recognized LEGO® Online Community) and preapring special printed bricks for events he was part of was always something that was truly appreciated by the whole community. Today, Marc-Andre is able to combine his passion for print and LEGO. He brings to the table an attention to detail, an artistic view, an understanding of the community and of copyright and brand respect.
